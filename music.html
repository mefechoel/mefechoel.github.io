Nicht Mehr

Nicht Mehr (en: Not any more) is the first EP I released. It features three instrumental songs. The sounds are a blend of electronic instruments, organic samples, synthesizers, electric guitar and acoustic as well as electronic drums. To my ears, the EP has a relaxed, atmospheric indie vibe.

Prag

Prag (en: Prague) is the first single I released. It is an instrumental song featuring heavy electric guitars and drums, with an atmospheric progressive rock/metal sound. It is inspired by the cold and foggy atmosphere around Prague's riverside and hills during winter.
